Why We Built It

One screen for what the family needs to know today.

Family calendars are usually scattered across three apps and a fridge whiteboard. What's Going On pulls everything that matters today into one quiet, glanceable view — and keeps it in sync between everyone's phones.

📅

Yesterday, today, tomorrow

Three pages. Swipe between them. See what just happened, what's happening now, and what's coming — without scrubbing through a month view.

🔁

Live family sync

Everyone in the family sees the same calendar, shopping list, and to-dos. Changes show up on every device within seconds.

🛒

Shared shopping & to-dos

One shopping list for the whole household. A separate "Dad to-dos" list for the things only one person remembers.

📖

Recipe holder

Save recipes by URL and pull their ingredients into the shopping list with one tap.

☁️

Live weather

Recurring chores

Features

What's in the preview.

  • Three-day window: Yesterday, today, and tomorrow pages with swipe navigation. No calendar grid, no month view — just the days that matter right now.
  • Manual + calendar items: Add a one-off task ("Bring oranges to soccer") or subscribe to a Google Calendar via iCal. Both show up together, sorted by time.
  • Family members & colors: Each family member gets a colored dot. Tag items to a person so it's clear who they're for.
  • Tags: Group items by category (school, work, kids, errands) with custom colors.
  • Recurring tasks: Daily, weekly, biweekly, or weekdays-only recurrence with smart skip handling.
  • Pin important items: Stick today's most important task to the top.
  • Shopping list: One shared list. Tap to mark bought, swipe to delete, drag to reorder. Active and done items are separated.
  • Dad to-dos: A second, separate list for chores that need one person's attention.
  • Recipe holder: Save recipes with URLs, pull ingredients into the shopping list.
  • Live weather: Today's conditions, high/low, and tomorrow's preview from WeatherKit.
  • Family sharing via code: 6-character invite codes; rotate at any time; remove members with one tap.
  • Background sync: Calendar refresh runs on iOS background tasks so the morning view is current when you open the app.
🔒

Privacy

Anonymous family sync — no name, email, or phone required. Family data is shared only with members of your group.
