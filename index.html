Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. Here's exactly how our apps handle your data.

Last updated: March 19, 2026

Overview

Setlist Builder is developed by drsulliv for LH Vantage®. This privacy policy describes how the app handles your information.

Data We Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data. All preferences and playlist information remain locally on your device. No data is sent to remote servers operated by LH Vantage.

Third-Party Services

The app interfaces with the following services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Apple Music — Used to create and manage playlists in your library via the native MusicKit framework.
  • YouTube / Google — Used for YouTube Music playlist creation via the YouTube Data API v3. Requires Google sign-in with YouTube scope.
  • Setlist.fm — Used to retrieve concert setlist data. No user account required.

Permissions

The app may request the following permissions:

  • Apple Music Library Access — To create playlists in your Apple Music library.
  • Network Access — To fetch setlist data and communicate with music platforms.
  • Google Sign-In (optional) — To authenticate with YouTube Music for playlist creation.

You may revoke these permissions at any time through your device settings.

Data Deletion

Since no personal data is collected or stored on our servers, you can delete all local app data by uninstalling the app or clearing app data through your device's Settings.
